[Coronaviruses](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Coronavirus) are a group of related viruses that cause respiratory tract infections. One such virus COVID-19 was the cause of a major world wide pandemic that really started to have a large impact in early 2020.
If you suspect that you or anyone you know has this virus please seek medical advice since this has the potential to be a serious health issue that can result in death if not treated.
